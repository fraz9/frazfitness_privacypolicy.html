Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to FrazFitness App. We are committed to respecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our FrazFitness App.

2. Data Collection and Use

User-Provided Information:

We collect data that you provide directly to us. This includes the bug reports and feature recommendations you submit through our app. The information you provide in these reports and recommendations may include text that describes your suggestions or issues encountered.

Exercise Data:

The app allows you to store your exercise data locally on your device. This data includes but is not limited to your workout records and progress.

Settings/Preferences:

Your app settings or preferences are stored locally on your device.

3. Data Export and Import

Exercise Data:

You have the option to export and import your exercise data in CSV format. This feature enables you to manage your data according to your preference.

Settings/Preferences:

Your app settings or preferences can be exported and imported in JSON format.

4. Use of Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the purpose of improving the app and addressing user-reported issues. We do not use this information for advertising or for sharing with third parties for commercial purposes.

5. Data Storage

The information you submit, along with your exercise data and app settings, is stored securely on your device. We do not transmit or store this data on any external servers.

6. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously and recommend that you regularly back up your data.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data locally on your device. You have full control over this data, including the ability to delete or export it as needed.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to make changes to this privacy policy at any time. If we make a significant change in our privacy practices, we will notify you of these changes by posting them on our app and/or website and indicating at the top of this policy the date it was last updated.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at frazfitness@hotmail.com.
